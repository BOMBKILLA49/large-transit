Transport Your Load with Confidence

Oversize transportation—whether hauling massive wind turbine blades slicing through highways, enormous precast bridge girders, towering power transformers, or sprawling aircraft fuselages—naturally carries heightened risks, from sudden unpredictable weather disruptions and frustrating port delays to potential handling incidents along the route; that's why our specialized Marine Insurance Coordination service steps in to safeguard your valuable cargo every step of the journey, partnering with trusted global insurers to deliver customized, tailored coverage precisely matched to your unique shipping requirements, cargo value, and final destination.

Protecting your oversize investment isn't optional—it's essential. Let us handle the insurance coordination so you can focus on what matters most: getting your extraordinary load to its destination safely and on time.

Service Highlights:

  • All-Risk Cargo InsuranceFull protection against loss, damage, theft, or natural events during oversize transit—critical for high-value, hard-to-replace loads like wind turbine blades or massive bridge girders.
  • Custom Coverage PlansInsurance tailored to the enormous value, unique dimensions, and specific sensitivities of your oversize cargo, from towering power transformers to sprawling aircraft fuselages.
  • Efficient Claims HandlingWe support you through the claims process with proper documentation, route-specific evidence, and timely follow-ups to minimize downtime on your heavy-haul projects.
  • Regulatory ComplianceGuidance to ensure your coverage meets all international shipping standards, including those required for oversize/overweight permits and specialized marine transport.
  • Flexible Insurance OptionsAvailable for single oversize shipments or ongoing heavy-haul contracts, with scalable premiums that align with your project scale and frequency.

Designed specifically for the complexities of oversize loads—whether navigating highways with wind turbine blades, coordinating marine legs for power transformers, or handling international routes for aerospace components—our Marine Insurance Coordination service delivers peace of mind, so you can focus on safe, on-time delivery of your extraordinary cargo.

Key Features:

  • Full cargo value protection for even the highest-value oversize shipments
  • Coverage for specialized oversize modes including FCL, LCL, breakbulk, and heavy-lift marine transport
  • Claims support and detailed documentation assistance tailored to oversize incidents
  • Risk assessment based on cargo type, dimensions, route challenges, and oversize permit requirements
  • Optional add-ons including natural disaster, war risk, strikes, and handling incidents common in heavy-haul operations

With our Insurance Coordination, your cargo is backed by more than just expertise—it's protected by precision, planning, and global insurance partners.
